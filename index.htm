About

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Princeton University working with Prof. Mengdi Wang. I got my Ph.D. (2014-2019) in the Department of Statistics at Purdue University advised by Prof. Guang Cheng. I am currently visiting Simons Institute for the Theory of Reinforcement Learning program in Fall 2020.

I will be joining Deepmind in London as a Research Scientist.

Previously, I was a research intern at Deepmind in London mentored by Dr. Tor Lattimore and Dr. Csaba Szepesvári (Summer, 2019), and a research intern at Adobe Research in San Jose mentored by Dr. Zheng Wen (Summer, 2018).

My research interests focus on the statistical perpective of reinforcement learning and multi-armed bandits. Previously, I worked on statistical machine learning and tensor learning.
