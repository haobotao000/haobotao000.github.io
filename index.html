About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Electrical Engineering at Princeton University working with Prof. Mengdi Wang. I got my Ph.D. (2014-2019) in the Department of Statistics at Purdue University advised by Prof. Guang Cheng, and B.S. (2010-2014) in the School of Mathematics at Nankai University.

Previously, I was a research intern at Deepmind in London mentored by Dr. Tor Lattimore and Dr. Csaba Szepesvári (Summer, 2019), and a research intern at Adobe Research in San Jose mentored by Dr. Zheng Wen (Summer, 2018).
